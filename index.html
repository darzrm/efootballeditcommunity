Home

Welcome to our official website. eFootball Edit Community (EEC) functions as a hub for designers, especially those focused on eFootball. Both experienced professionals and newcomers are invited to join.

In this community, members share assets, exchange editing techniques, and discuss eFootball updates in an open and supportive environment. We uphold collaboration so everyone can grow together. Please join us and contribute to this community.

Community Rules

All members must keep order by avoiding spam, provoking conflict especially on religion politics ethnicity, and sharing NSFW content. Personal promotion is not allowed; avoid attacking or belittling others and keep discussions on topic particularly in the main group.

We also expect everyone to respect others work: do not take steal or claim others creations. In all interactions communicate politely uphold etiquette and prioritize humane values to keep the community safe and comfortable.

Events

Daily Challenge is a challenge that may be held at any time. It offers rewards where first place earns 3 points, second place gets 2 points, and third place receives 1 point.

Monthly Challenge is a challenge held once every month. It offers higher rewards compared to the daily challenge, where first place earns 9 points, second place gets 6 points, and third place receives 3 points.

Special Challenge is a unique challenge held during special occasions such as major events, new year celebrations, community anniversaries, or special collaborations. Due to its exclusive nature, it offers higher rewards: 15 points for first place, 10 points for second place, and 5 points for third place.

The participant ID is taken from the last four digits of their WhatsApp number and is used as a unique identifier for each participant.
