About us

eFootball Edit Community is a WhatsApp-based community open to everyone who has an interest in card editing, playing the eFootball game, and a passion for football. It serves as a gathering place for creators and players to share, learn, and grow together in an active and enjoyable environment.

Within this community, there are many exciting events regularly organized by the admins that can be joined by all members. These events provide opportunities for members to showcase their skills, compete in a healthy way, and win various attractive rewards.

eFootball Edit Community is also known as a positive and supportive community. Members help each other, share tips, and can join editing classes provided to improve their skills. In addition, there are various free assets available to support creativity in creating card designs.

With a positive atmosphere, engaging events, and strong member support, eFootball Edit Community is the perfect place for anyone who wants to grow in the world of editing and eFootball while enjoying a solid and friendly community.

Rules

All members are required to maintain order by refraining from any form of spamming. Members are also prohibited from provoking conflicts, especially those related to sensitive issues such as religion, politics, race, and similar topics. Sharing NSFW content (18+ and related material) is strictly not allowed in this group.

In addition, members are expected to avoid creating conflict or engaging in behavior that attacks, insults, or demeans others. Discussions must remain focused and limited to the designated topics, particularly within the main group. Any form of personal promotion is also not permitted.

All members must respect the work of others by not taking, stealing, claiming, or committing any unethical actions toward other members’ creations. In all interactions, members are expected to communicate respectfully, uphold proper etiquette, and prioritize humane values to maintain a safe and comfortable community environment.
