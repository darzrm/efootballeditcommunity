Home

Welcome to our official website. eFootball Edit Community (EEC) functions as a hub for designers, especially those focused on eFootball. Both experienced professionals and newcomers are invited to join.

In this community, members share assets, exchange editing techniques, and discuss eFootball updates in an open and supportive environment. We uphold collaboration so everyone can grow together. Please join us and contribute to this community.

Community Rules

All members must keep order by avoiding spam, provoking conflict especially on religion politics ethnicity, and sharing NSFW content. Personal promotion is not allowed; avoid attacking or belittling others and keep discussions on topic particularly in the main group.

We also expect everyone to respect others work: do not take steal or claim others creations. In all interactions communicate politely uphold etiquette and prioritize humane values to keep the community safe and comfortable.

More Information

  • Vission and mission

    EEC aims to become a creative, active, and inspiring eFootball editing community. The community focuses on skill development, collaboration, and innovation in the world of design and editing. EEC’s mission is to develop members’ abilities, provide a space for knowledge sharing, encourage creativity through works, and build a solid and positive community environment.

  • Activities

    EEC offers various activities designed to improve members’ skills and creativity, such as design challenges, editing events, and collaborative projects. In addition, the community also holds sharing sessions and discussions to exchange knowledge and experiences in eFootball editing. Each program is created to help members grow consistently while enjoying the creative process.

  • Recruitment

    EEC is open to anyone who has an interest in eFootball editing and design. The joining process is carried out through available community platforms, and there are opportunities to participate in division recruitment for members who want to contribute more. The community seeks individuals who are active, creative, and eager to grow together.

  • Communication

    EEC provides various communication platforms such as WhatsApp and other social media to facilitate interaction among members. All information related to events, announcements, and community updates will be delivered in a structured manner through these platforms. With active communication, every member can stay connected and keep up with the community’s development.
